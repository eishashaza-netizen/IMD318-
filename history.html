Our History

The journey of ZUS Coffee in transforming Malaysia's coffee culture.

2019 - Foundation

ZUS Coffee was established in Malaysia with a mission to provide premium-quality coffee at affordable prices. The company aimed to make good coffee accessible to everyone.

2020 - Digital Innovation

ZUS Coffee introduced digital ordering through its mobile application, allowing customers to enjoy a more convenient coffee-buying experience.

2021 - Rapid Expansion

The brand expanded rapidly across major cities in Malaysia and became one of the fastest-growing coffee chains in the country.

2022 - Strong Market Presence

With increasing customer demand, ZUS Coffee continued opening new outlets and strengthening its position in the Malaysian coffee industry.

2023 - Nationwide Recognition

The brand gained nationwide popularity through innovative marketing, mobile promotions, and high customer satisfaction.

Present Day

Today, ZUS Coffee serves millions of customers through its extensive network of outlets and remains committed to innovation, affordability, and premium coffee experiences.
