About Me ♡

Assalamualaikum & hi! My name is Shazanira, I'm 21 years old & I'm from Perak. This section is supposed to tell you about me, but the truth is I’m probably still figuring that out myself. I’m at that stage of life where I’m technically an adult, but sometimes I still need a minute to process simple decisions. I say “it’ll only take five minutes” a lot, and somehow it never takes five minutes. Saya juga mempunyai bakat semula jadi untuk menangguhkan perkara penting dan kemudian terkejut apabila perkara itu masih belum siap. Most of my days consist of doing what needs to be done, avoiding what I don’t want to do, and then eventually doing it anyway. It’s a system. Not a good system, but a system. Other than that, I’m just a normal person with random thoughts, random interests, and a collection of embarrassing memories that occasionally decide to replay themselves at 2 a.m. Thanks for visiting my website. I hope you’re having a better day than my Wi-Fi connection.
